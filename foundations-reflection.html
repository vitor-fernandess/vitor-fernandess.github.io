core reflection

The core activities during the foundations was fundamental for my learning and personal growth to become a better person and a better programmer. Learning concepts like EQ and self-awareness was extremely helpful to make myself aware that my values can affect people around me positively, and or negatively.

three new things you have learnt about yourself and your ego due to the core learning?


The core learning during foundations opened my mind about myself in a few ways. It made me realize that asking for help is normal and it does not make people think that I am not smart. It also made me notice that the energy that I have can affect other people around me.

the roles of values, empathy, and self-awareness in learning and programming?


Values, empathy and self-awareness can help me and the people around me to have a smoother workflow, because I am aware of my emotional state and how it might affect people that I am working with.

What has surprised you the most about core learning?


I was not expecting to study EQ while studying coding. It definitely changed my whole perspective on how to work.

What were the most challenging aspects of the core learning?


Self-Awareness. I believe most people tend to avoid or mask their feelings, which can be bad for themselves and the people around them.

are spending so much time focusing on core learning in a web development Bootcamp course?


I believe that one of the values that Dev Academy has is to make better people and better programmers at the same time.

Does the time you spent studying core learning here feel like a waste of time?


Not at all. It definitely made me realize a couple things that I had to learn about myself, and also how to work better with others. Challenges like “listeting and looping”, “pair programming”, and “Introduce yourself” are extremely important to shape a better programmer and a better person.
