te whare tapa wha

Te whare tapa wha is a model of health created by Mason Durie in 1984. The model is described as a house with 4 walls. These walls represent Taha Tiana (Physical wellbeing), Taha Whanau (Family wellbeing), Taha Hinengaro (emotional and mental wellbeing), Taha Wairua(Spiritual wellbeing), and Whenua(Interconections to the land and environment).

taha tinana(physical wellbeing)


Doing 7km runs before class twice a week and practicing football after class twice a week.

taha whanau(family wellbeing)


Video call with my family three times a week.

Taha hinengaro(emotional and mental wellbeing)


Meditation every morning and night, music sessions once a week.

Taha wairua(Spiritual wellbeing)


Praying everyday.
