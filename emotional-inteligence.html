Emotional inteligence

Emotional intelligence is simply the ability to manage both your own emotions and understand the emotions of people around you.


The defference between IQ and EQ


IQ is a measure of your ability to solve problems and think logically, whereas EQ measures your ability to understand and manage your emotions.

Why is EQ important


EQ is important to build relationships, reduce team stress, defuse conflict and improve job satisfaction.
